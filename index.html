Why?

Edu-Alchem

Empowering Educators with Data insights

It is on understanding the fact that, any education that treats everyone the same way is the most unfair of educational system the evolution of edu-alchem began. In a conventional classroom, where each of kids will have different understanding and conception of the same concept it is important we look for tools to empower the educators through modern day gold, Data for cognitive and insightful learning.

Edu-alchem Tablet Laboratory

Edu-Alchem Tablet Laboratory is an attempt to bring in the latest of technology into the classroom to give individual attention and space for a kid to learn and explore all by themselves. By playing games catering to concepts within their curriculum and more the tablet laboratory is to open a whole new window of possibilities a textbook, or an educator can ever offer
